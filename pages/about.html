MIT Kickboxing Club

The MIT Kickboxing Club is a MIT Club Sport for those interested in all levels of kickboxing.

Our club is not tied to one particular form of martial art, but incorporates elements from various styles of stand up fighting. In addition to hand, foot, elbow, and knee strikes, we also work take-downs and defense skills in order to work up to sparring.

So please, JOIN US anytime by coming to our workouts! To participate, you need to be either a current student or staff member at MIT, or an alumnus, or the spouse of any of those. Come dressed in work out wear (shorts, t-shirt) and if possible, bring a mouthguard (and cup).
